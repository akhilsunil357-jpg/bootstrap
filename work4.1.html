Frequently Asked Question

How to recover your passward?
Password recovery allows users to regain access to their account if they forget their password. The user clicks on the “Forgot Password” option on the login page. A reset link or verification code is sent to the registered email or phone number.
How can I track my order?
Order tracking allows customers to check the current status of their purchase. The user logs into their account and selects the “My Orders” or “Track Order” option. Real-time updates show whether the order is confirmed, shipped, or delivered.
About refund policy
The refund policy explains the conditions under which customers can request a refund. Refunds are processed if the order is canceled, damaged, or not delivered as promised. The refunded amount is credited back to the original payment method within a specified time.
